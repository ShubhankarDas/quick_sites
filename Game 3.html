Write Your Gut a Letter 💌

5 minutes. Private. No wrong words.

Your gut has been sending you messages — probably for a while. This is your chance to write back.

You'll have 5 minutes and 4 gentle prompts. Everything you write is completely private — you'll never be asked to read it aloud unless you choose to. At the end, you'll see your letter in full.
